Legs Up the Wall (Viparita Karani)

Dosha: Vata | Difficulty: Beginner | Duration: 10-15 minutes

A gentle inversion that helps calm Vata energy and reduce anxiety. Perfect for evening practice.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1Sit close to a wall
2Swing legs up the wall
3Lie back and rest arms by sides
4Close eyes and breathe deeply
5Hold for 10-15 minutes
6Bend knees and roll to side to exit

Benefits

  • Calms the nervous system
  • Reduces anxiety
  • Improves circulation
  • Relieves tired legs
  • Grounds Vata energy

Contraindications

  • Glaucoma
  • High blood pressure
  • Neck problems

Dosha-Specific Instructions

Vata: Excellent for calming and grounding. Practice daily for best results.

Pitta: Cooling and soothing. Good for reducing heat and irritation.

Kapha: May feel too passive. Combine with gentle movement.
