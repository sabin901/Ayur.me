Seated Forward Bend (Paschimottanasana)

Dosha: Pitta | Difficulty: Beginner | Duration: 5-10 minutes

A cooling forward bend that helps balance Pitta energy and reduce heat in the body and mind.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1Sit with legs extended forward
2Inhale and lengthen spine
3Exhale and fold forward from hips
4Reach for feet, ankles, or shins
5Keep spine long, don't round back
6Hold for 5-10 minutes with deep breathing

Benefits

  • Cools and soothes Pitta
  • Calms the mind
  • Stretches spine and hamstrings
  • Reduces anger and irritability
  • Improves digestion

Contraindications

  • Severe back problems
  • Pregnancy (modify)
  • Herniated disc

Dosha-Specific Instructions

Vata: Be gentle and use props. Don't force the stretch.

Pitta: Excellent for cooling and calming. Practice in cool environment.

Kapha: Good for opening back body. May need to hold longer.
