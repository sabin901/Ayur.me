Sun Salutation (Surya Namaskara)

Dosha: Kapha | Difficulty: Beginner | Duration: 10-15 minutes

A dynamic sequence that helps energize Kapha and stimulate metabolism. Perfect for morning practice.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1Stand in Mountain Pose
2Inhale, raise arms overhead
3Exhale, fold forward
4Inhale, half lift
5Exhale, step back to plank
6Lower to Chaturanga
7Inhale, upward dog
8Exhale, downward dog
9Step forward, half lift
10Exhale, fold forward
11Inhale, rise up
12Repeat 5-10 rounds

Benefits

  • Stimulates metabolism
  • Energizes Kapha
  • Improves circulation
  • Builds heat and energy
  • Strengthens entire body

Contraindications

  • High blood pressure
  • Heart conditions
  • Pregnancy (modify)
  • Severe back problems

Dosha-Specific Instructions

Vata: Practice slowly and mindfully. Don't rush.

Pitta: Practice in cool environment. Avoid overexertion.

Kapha: Excellent for energizing. Practice vigorously and regularly.
