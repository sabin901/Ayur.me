Warrior I (Virabhadrasana I)

Dosha: Kapha | Difficulty: Beginner | Duration: 30 seconds - 2 minutes each side

A powerful standing pose that builds strength and energy, perfect for stimulating Kapha.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1Start in Mountain Pose
2Step left foot back 3-4 feet
3Turn left foot 45 degrees
4Bend right knee over ankle
5Raise arms overhead
6Look up slightly
7Hold for 30 seconds - 2 minutes
8Repeat on other side

Benefits

  • Builds strength and stamina
  • Energizes Kapha
  • Improves focus and determination
  • Strengthens legs and core
  • Opens chest and shoulders

Contraindications

  • High blood pressure
  • Heart problems
  • Knee injuries

Dosha-Specific Instructions

Vata: Be gentle and don't overextend. Use props if needed.

Pitta: Practice in cool environment. Focus on breath.

Kapha: Excellent for building energy. Hold longer and practice regularly.
