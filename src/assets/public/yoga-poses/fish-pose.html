Fish Pose (Matsyasana)

Dosha: Pitta | Difficulty: Intermediate | Duration: 3-5 minutes

A gentle backbend that opens the heart and helps cool Pitta energy while improving breathing.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1Lie on back with legs extended
2Place hands under hips, palms down
3Press elbows into floor
4Lift chest and arch back
5Rest crown of head on floor
6Breathe deeply and hold for 3-5 minutes

Benefits

  • Opens chest and heart
  • Cools and soothes Pitta
  • Improves breathing
  • Reduces anger and frustration
  • Stretches throat and neck

Contraindications

  • Neck problems
  • High blood pressure
  • Migraine

Dosha-Specific Instructions

Vata: Be gentle and use props. Don't overextend.

Pitta: Excellent for cooling and opening heart. Practice regularly.

Kapha: Good for opening chest. May need support under back.
