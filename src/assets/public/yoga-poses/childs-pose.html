Child's Pose (Balasana)

Dosha: Vata | Difficulty: Beginner | Duration: 5-10 minutes

A gentle resting pose that helps ground Vata energy and calm the nervous system. Perfect for reducing anxiety and stress.

Step-by-Step Instructions

1Start on hands and knees
2Sit back on your heels
3Lower your torso between your thighs
4Extend arms forward or alongside body
5Rest forehead on the mat
6Breathe deeply and hold for 5-10 minutes

Benefits

  • Calms the nervous system
  • Reduces anxiety and stress
  • Grounds Vata energy
  • Stretches hips and thighs
  • Relieves back pain

Contraindications

  • Knee injuries
  • Ankle problems
  • Pregnancy (modify with knees apart)

Dosha-Specific Instructions

Vata: Excellent for grounding and calming Vata energy. Practice slowly and hold longer.

Pitta: Can be cooling but avoid if it causes frustration. Use props for comfort.

Kapha: Good for opening chest and shoulders. May need to hold shorter duration.
